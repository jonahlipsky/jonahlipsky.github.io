welcome to my website

Hello, My name is Jonah, I'm a software engineer currently based in New York city.

I'm a full-stack software engineer with a specialization in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. You can find my résumé here.

I'm particularly interested in the transformation to society that is underway as a result of Artificial Intelligence. I think about A.I. from multiple angles, including: as a software developer, as a data scientist, as a student of psychology, and as someone engaged in culture through the lens of the arts.

After graduating from Bennington College in 2013, I worked for several years at the Clinical and Affective Neuroscience Lab at Brown University as a Lab Manager and Research Assistant, where we studied the neuroscience and psychology of meditation and mindfulness.

I believe we need to think about A.I. from many different angles in order to fully understand its impact to society and how to guide its emergence. I have recently been posting some of my thoughts about A.I. on my LinkedIn.

Check out some of my coding projects below, and I'm excited to hear from you!

Academic Bio and Bibliography

From 2014 - 2018, before I became a coder, I worked as a Lab Manager and Research Assistant at the Clinical and Affective Neuroscience Lab (CLAN) at Brown University. Housed in the Warren Alpert Medical School, the CLAN lab studies the Clinical Psychology and Neuroscience of Meditation. I managed 50+ undergraduates and graduate students working on 15+ projects. I ran many aspects of a clinical trial, participated in a systematic review that led to a meta-analysis and several publications, and leveraged my budding coding skills to contribute to our projects. Here are my four publications that came out of that experience.
