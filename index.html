Coding, Math, and Machine Learning

For the past 4+ years I have been working at Medidata Solutions, the leading provider of software for companies running Clinical Research Trials. During this time, our industry has undergone a shift towards Machine Learning and A.I. as core to products and services. While this transformation was well underway when I joined the industry 5 years ago, it now defines it.

During my time at Medidata, I have established myself as a talented engineer and team leader, which you can see from my two promotions. In parallel to this foundational on-the-job education in web development, I have pursued an education in Mathematics and Machine Learning. I took a series of three courses on the Mathematics for Machine Learning and followed up with Andrew Ng's Mathematics for Machine Learning Specialization (see certificates on LinkedIn).

In 2022, I embarked on a POC of a Machine Learning model on the job at Medidata. This was an excellent real world experience. I trained a deep neural network, created synthetic features, and got my hands dirty with Python, NumPy, Pandas, and scikit-learn.

I am well-rounded when it comes to A.I. I have an undergraduate degree in Psychology and Drama -- so I'm interested in the mind. I've learned the Mathematics that underlies the models. I even wrote a play about A.I., to engage the material from a societal and humanistic perspective (see cutitouttheplay.com).
