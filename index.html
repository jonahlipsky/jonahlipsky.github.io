Academic Bio and Bibliography

From 2014 - 2018, before I became a coder, I worked as a Lab Manager and Research Assistant at the Clinical and Affective Neuroscience Lab (CLAN) at Brown University. Housed in the Warren Alpert Medical School, the CLAN lab studies the Clinical Psychology and Neuroscience of Meditation. I managed 50+ undergraduates and graduate students working on 15+ projects. I ran many aspects of a clinical trial, participated in a systematic review that led to a meta-analysis and several publications, and leveraged my budding coding skills to contribute to our projects.
