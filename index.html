My background in Coding and Data Science

I am a full stack web developer with a focus in backend web development. For the past 4+ years, I have been working at Medidata Solutions, the leading provider of software for companies running Clinical Research Trials. Promoted twice during my time there, I have established myself as a skilled engineer and team leader. At Medidata, I have straddled the worlds of full stack web development on the one hand and Software Design and Architecture on the other. I've designed features and microsservices and have followed through with their implementation.

In parallel to my day job, I have pursued continuing education in Mathematics and Machine Learning through Coursera and Harvard Extension school. I took a sequence of courses on the Mathematics for Machine Learning (through University College London) and then Machine Learning (Andrew Ng's courses). In 2022, I was excited to put this knowledge to use when I did a POC of a deep neural network for a business problem at Medidata. Currently, I am researching and developing a neural network to play the game of Go, inspired by Alpha Go.
